About

About Us:


Mike and Sally Santala started Double S Towing Service in 1998. The business started with two roll back trucks and one old winch truck. Mike and Sally have grown the business to the fleet it is today of light, medium and heavy wrecker tow trucks covering a large swath of Eastern Colorado. From Burlington to Limon, Double S towing has the equipment and years of service to get you out of any bind you find yourself on out on the roads.

Services

Services We Offer:


Double S Towing offers light, medium and heavy-duty wrecker service 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

We also offer roadside assistance including: Jump starts, out of gas calls, tire changes, lock outs, jump starts, load transfers, accident recoveries, and light and heavy truck repairs.

Fleet


Meet The Fleet:


Light-Med

Our light/medium duty roll back is a 2007 C6500 Chevy truck with a 22’ long, 102” wide chevron rollback bed. It features a side pulling secondary winch system that allows it to recover accidents off the side of the highway without the truck having to leave the road. The truck has 6 seats total to haul the family along with their vehicle. Fully equipped with tools and equipment to handle any recovery and roadside assistance issues that arise.

Med-Heavy

Our medium/heavy roll back truck is 1997 Freightliner FLD 112. It features a 33,000 lbs. truck capacity, with a 12,000-load capacity on a 22’ long, 102” wide century aluminum bed. The truck features an M11 Cummins engine that delivers 400hp to haul the heavy loads plus the ability to haul both bumper pull and goose neck trailers. The truck is fully equipped with tools and equipment to handle any recovery and roadside assistance issues that arise.

Heavy-Duty Wrecker

Our heavy-duty wrecker unit is built on a 1988 Peterbilt 379 with a CAT 3406 engine. This truck has a 40-ton extendable boom and a 50,000 lbs. underreach unit. The truck alone weighs 48,000, it is more than capable to handle any heavy-duty recovery it comes up against with ease. Other companies rely on two or three trucks to do what this one truck can do on a heavy-duty truck wreck. The truck is fully loaded with all lifting and recovery tools to handle any heavy-duty roadside obstacles that come its way.
